About Restech

Driving the global digital transformation of the real estate industry through innovative, secure, and scalable Restech solutions.

Welcome to Real Estate Software Technology Private Limited (Restech), a pioneering force in the global Real State industry. Headquartered in Jaipur, India, we specialize in delivering enterprise-grade digital infrastructure tailored exclusively for real estate businesses globally.

Our expert engineering team builds comprehensive digital ecosystems—including dynamic property portals, custom CRMs, intelligent mobile applications, and lead-routing API integrations—that empower brokers, agencies, and builders to scale seamlessly.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make modern digital technology accessible and affordable for every real estate business, helping them generate more leads, automate operations, and build stronger trust online.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted technology partner for the real estate sector, creating flawless software architectures that connect buyers, sellers, and agents globally.
